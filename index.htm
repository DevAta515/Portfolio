    Web development is the creation and maintenance of websites and web applications. Developers ensure cross-browser compatibility, optimize performance, and may work on server-side functionality.     Graphic design is the art of creating visual content to convey messages and ideas. Graphic designers use typography, imagery, and layout techniques to design everything from logos and advertisements to posters and websites.
